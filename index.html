Welcome to 2015!

Rails Girls Summer of Code started in 2013 and has been a growing success ever since. This year we will be back and hope to make another summer full of code happen with your help!

Rails Girls Summer of Code offers three month full-time scholarships to pairs of Rails Girls students, supported by coaches. This community-driven initiative is about learning by doing — and expanding upon knowledge and coding skills by contributing to a great Open Source project.

The Rails Girls Summer of Code program starts on the 1st of July 2015 and aims at providing sponsored scholarships to 20 students.

This cannot be done without help from our fantastic community. Please consider a donation or help out as a coach, coaching company or supporting organizer!
